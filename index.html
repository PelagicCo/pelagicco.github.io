Pelagic

WHAT WE DO

At Pelagic, we envision a future where fish meat for human consumption is grown outside of whole fish, in a more sanitary and humane environment. We envision a future where our fish consumption doesn't have to ruin one of the Earth's most important ecosystems.

The pelagic zone is the largest aquatic habitat on Earth, and where 11% of known fish species live. The overfishing of this area is one of many causes of the collapse of ocean ecosystems. If we are to find a way to move forward sustainably with humanity's fish consumption, we need to find a replacement for the pelagic zone.

This is where we come in.
